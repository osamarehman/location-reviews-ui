Location Reviews UI Components

This page demonstrates the usage of the Location Reviews UI components.

SimpleReviewCard

A simple, clean review card that displays reviewer information and content.

<div class="simple-review-card-container" 
     data-name="John Smith" 
     data-rating="5" 
     data-date="2023-05-15" 
     data-content="Great experience! The staff was professional and the facility was clean and modern."
     data-avatar="">
</div>

TestimonialSlider

A slider that displays multiple testimonials with navigation controls.

<div class="testimonial-slider-container" 
     data-testimonials='[
         {"name":"John Smith","rating":5,"date":"2023-05-15","content":"Great experience! The staff was professional and the facility was clean and modern.","avatar":""},
         {"name":"Sarah Johnson","rating":4,"date":"2023-04-22","content":"Very satisfied with my experience. The procedure was quick and the results were better than expected.","avatar":""},
         {"name":"Michael Brown","rating":5,"date":"2023-03-10","content":"I had a great experience at this location. The staff was friendly and the results were amazing.","avatar":""}
     ]'
     data-auto-play="true"
     data-interval="5000">
</div>

RatingsSummary

A component that displays a summary of ratings with average, total, and breakdown by star level.

<div class="ratings-summary-container" 
     data-average="4.5" 
     data-total="120"
     data-distribution='{"5":80,"4":20,"3":15,"2":3,"1":2}'>
</div>

ReviewForm

A form for submitting new reviews with name, email, rating, and content fields.

<div class="review-form-container" 
     data-location-id="123"
     data-submit-url=""
     data-action="submit_review"
     data-success-message="Thank you for your review!">
</div>
